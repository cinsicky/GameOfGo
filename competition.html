Competition

Chunlan Cup

The Chunlan Cup World Professional go Championship, founded in 1998, is a go competition jointly organized by the Chinese go Association and Jiangsu Chunlan Group.

Samsung Cup

Samsung Cup World Open Baduk Championship is an international go individual competition held in South Korea and one of the world go competitions. The event was founded in 1996.

Ing Cup

Ing Cup World Professional go Championship was founded in 1987 by the Chinese Taiwanese industrialist Ying Changqi in 1987. The first competition was held in Beijing in 1988, and held every four years, with the "go Olympic Games" reputation.

LG Cup

LG Cup World Chess King war is one of the major international competition. Its predecessor is the national game of chess in Korea, hosted by Korea daily, LG group sponsored.1996, and the sponsor has the trend of internationalization of weiqi, and founded the LG Cup World Chess King war.

Association

The American go Association was founded in 1935 and is headquartered in New York. Hundreds of regular competitions are held in the United States each year, and member players are counted.

Shanghai go Association was established by Shanghai Sports Bureau in 1996. Shanghai go Association is in charge of popularizing and improving chess items and related competitions and communicating at home and abroad.

The Chinese go Association is a non-profit, professional and professional mass organization composed of go administrators, professional chess players, amateur chess players and national go units.
